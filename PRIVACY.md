# Privacy Policy for Website Attention Cleaner

**Last Updated: January 9, 2026**

## Overview
Website Attention Cleaner is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection
We **DO NOT** collect:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Usage analytics
- Any identifiable data

## Data Storage
The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Your cleaning preferences (which distractions to hide)
- Focus mode state (on/off)
- Statistics (time focused, distractions blocked, sessions completed)
- Reading mode preferences
- Temporary timer settings

All data is stored using Chrome's storage.sync API, which:
- Syncs across your Chrome browsers if you're signed in
- Remains under your control
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions Used
- **storage**: To save your preferences and statistics
- **activeTab**: To apply cleaning rules to the current website
- **scripting**: To inject content scripts that remove distracting elements

## Third-Party Services
This extension does **NOT**:
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics services
- Include tracking pixels
- Connect to any third-party APIs

## Data Deletion
To delete all data:
1. Uninstall the extension, OR
2. Click "Reset Statistics" in the extension popup, OR
3. Clear Chrome extension data in chrome://settings/content/cookies

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
