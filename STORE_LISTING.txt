=== DETAILED DESCRIPTION ===
Stay focused and productive by removing distracting elements from your favorite websites.

Website Attention Cleaner intelligently removes ads, recommendations, sidebars, shorts, and other distractions so you can focus on the content that matters to you.

?? KEY FEATURES:
• One-click Focus Mode toggle
• Customizable cleaning options (choose what to hide)
• Statistics dashboard to track your productivity
• Reading Mode for better text readability
• Temporary focus sessions (15/30/60 minutes)
• Keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+F) for quick toggling
• Per-site preferences that sync across devices

?? SUPPORTED WEBSITES:
• YouTube - Hide shorts, recommendations, comments, ads
• Reddit - Remove sidebar, trending posts, promoted content
• Twitter/X - Hide trending sidebar, promoted tweets
• Instagram - Remove suggested posts, sponsored content
• Facebook - Hide suggested posts, stories, ads
• LinkedIn - Remove ads, recommendations, sidebar
• TikTok - Hide recommendations, ads
• Medium - Remove popups, claps, "More stories"
• News sites - Generic cleaning for ads, sidebars, popups

?? PRODUCTIVITY TRACKING:
• Total time spent in focus mode
• Number of distractions blocked
• Sessions completed
• Average session insights

?? PRIVACY FIRST:
• No data collection
• No tracking or analytics
• Everything stored locally on your device
• Open source and transparent

Perfect for students, professionals, writers, researchers, or anyone who wants to minimize distractions and maximize productivity online.
